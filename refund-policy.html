Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Timeout Restaurant, Garki, Abuja

At Timeout Restaurant, we are committed to providing you with fresh, high-quality African and Continental meals with every order. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle returns, complaints, and refunds.

1 General Policy

All food and beverage orders placed through Timeout Restaurant are considered final upon preparation. Due to the perishable nature of food items, we are unable to accept returns once an order has been prepared and dispatched.

2 Eligible Refund Situations

We will consider a refund or replacement in the following circumstances:

  • Your order arrived with missing items
  • You received an incorrect item that differs from what was ordered
  • Your order arrived in an unacceptable condition (spoiled, damaged, or contaminated)
  • Your order was not delivered within the stated timeframe with no prior notice

3 How to Request a Refund

To be eligible for a refund or replacement, you must:

  • Contact us within 1 hour of receiving your order
  • Provide your order details (name, order time, items ordered)
  • Describe the issue clearly — photos are helpful where applicable

Requests made after 1 hour of delivery may not be eligible for a refund.

4 Refund Processing

Once your refund request is reviewed and approved:

  • Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days
  • Refunds will be returned via the original payment method where applicable
  • We may offer a replacement meal or store credit at our discretion

5 Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after an order has been prepared
  • Incorrect delivery address provided by the customer
  • Delays caused by circumstances beyond our control (traffic, weather, etc.)
  • Orders that have been partially consumed

6 Delivery Hours

Timeout Restaurant operates and delivers from 9:00 AM – 11:30 PM daily. Orders placed outside these hours will be processed on the next available delivery window.
